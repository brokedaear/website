## 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our products and services effectively. This includes:

- **Name**: To identify your account and personalize our communications.
- **Email Address**: To send you updates, order confirmations, and support-related messages.
- **Purchase and Download Records**: To manage your licensed software and assist you when needed.

- Information provided when creating an account or placing an order is retained indefinitely unless you request its deletion.

- Our website does not currently use cookies to collect personal data.

## 2. Legal Basis for Processing Data

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

- **Contractual Necessity**: To fulfill our obligations when you purchase a product or service.
- **Consent**: When you explicitly opt-in for marketing communications or other optional services.
- **Legitimate Interests**: To ensure the functionality of our website and provide a seamless user experience.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

- Process your orders and deliver our products and services.
- Communicate with you about your account, purchases, and support needs.
- Ensure the proper operation of our online store and the software you have licensed from us.

Your data will not be used for marketing purposes unless you have explicitly chosen to receive marketing communications.


## 4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

- When it is required to provide our services (e.g., payment processors, shipping providers).
- When mandated by law or to comply with legal obligations.

We will never sell your information to any third party.

Broke da EAR! operates from the United States. If you are accessing our website from outside the U.S., please note that your data will be transferred to and processed on our servers located in the United States. We take appropriate measures to protect your data during international transfers, such as relying on standard contractual clauses or other legally accepted safeguards.

You have control over your personal data, including the following rights:

- **Access**: Request a comprehensive review of the data we have collected about you.
- **Deletion**: Ask us to remove all information we have about you.
- **Correction**: Request updates or corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
- **Portability**: Obtain a copy of your data in a commonly used format.
- **Objection**: Object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
- **Restriction**: Request a limitation on the processing of your data in specific cases.

To exercise these rights, contact us at **data@brokedaear.com**
